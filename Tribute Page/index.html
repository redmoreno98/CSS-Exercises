Lapu-Lapu

Lapu-Lapu, the first successful defender of the Philippines.

About the Legend

☛ LapuLapu Dimantag, born in 1491 in Mactan, Philippines, was one of the two datus of Mactan before the Spanish arrived.

☛ He is famously known for the Battle of Mactan on April 26, 1512, where he and his men defeated the Spanish forces led by Ferdinand Magellan.

☛ Lapu-Lapu is revered as the first national hero of the Philippines for his resistance against Spanish colonization.

☛ He is also known for his bravery, leadership, and patriotism.

☛ Lapu-Lapu is considered the 3,020th most popular politician and the 4th most popular biography from the Philippines.

☛ In 2017, April 27th was declared "Lapu Lapu day" to commemorate his victory in the Battle of Mactan.

☛ A type of grouper and a city were also named after him.
